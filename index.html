About AOS Venture

A Residential Platform Built on Execution, Not Speculation

AOS Venture was built on one belief: the most reliable path to long-term wealth in real estate runs through disciplined residential investing. We focus exclusively on single family and multifamily residential assets — the markets we know best, where our operational edge creates the most value.

We are operators first. From sourcing off-market single family deals to developing small multifamily infill projects, every decision at AOS Venture is grounded in conservative underwriting, hands-on management, and a commitment to building a lasting residential portfolio.

For-Sale Residential Subdivisions

We take raw and infill land from entitlement through vertical construction and retail sale — building well-designed, for-sale single family communities and capturing the full development margin in-house.

Our subdivision platform controls every lever that drives a project’s return: securing the right entitlements, engineering the highest compliant lot yield, designing market-leading for-sale product, and managing construction and sales all the way to closing.
